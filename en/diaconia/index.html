Unification & North Korea Ministry

Vision

A priestly nation where North and South Korea are united as one, freely worshiping God and serving the world

Ministry Focus

Going beyond economic logic to focus on the restoration of human dignity and the restoration of worship.

Ongoing Ministries

  • Lectures on understanding North Korea and reunification
  • Supporting rescue of North Korean residents and educational institutions for North Korean defectors
  • Intercessory prayer for reunification

Programs

  • Book club on North Korea and reunification topics
  • Special lectures and academy on reunification

Partner Organizations

Woori Children International & Tree of Life Yeomyung School South-North Love Network Bulssi Mission Majungmul Woori-Duri-Hana Center TWR Korea Northern Mission Christian Unification Studies Association

International Student Ministry

"Love the Lord your God with all your heart and with all your soul and with all your strength and with all your mind; and love your neighbor as yourself. — Luke 10:27"

Vision

To be a friend to those living far from home, building up internationals through the love and gospel of Christ, and sending them back to their home countries

Ministry Focus

Sharing God's love through Korean language education and cultural experiences.

Programs

  • Korean cultural experience classes twice a month (during the semester)
  • One-on-one Korean language classes (during school breaks)

Social Service Ministry

"Whatever you did for one of the least of these brothers and sisters of mine, you did for me. — Matthew 25:40"

Vision

Supporting those in need in society with the heart of Jesus, who seeks out and cares for the vulnerable

Ongoing Ministries

  • Operating the Love Letter / Poetry Jar at Anam Station in partnership with the Traffic Culture Mission Council
  • Supporting vulnerable groups through the Anam-dong Community Welfare Council
  • Delivering coal briquettes to basic livelihood recipients and near-poor households in partnership with Warm Korean Peninsula
