Rodemnamu Church hopes that all young adults will become youth of Jesus with the dreams and vision of God's Kingdom

Community

We help young adults in this generation experience the work of holistic recovery through the gospel and the power of the Holy Spirit. We raise young adults as disciples of Jesus with a shepherd's heart — as healthy citizens of God's Kingdom, as co-workers building His Kingdom in every sphere of family and society, and as witnesses of the gospel sent to the ends of the earth

Photo

A Community That Worships Together

We are a worship community that worships God the Father in the Word and the Spirit. Through monthly communion, we experience the grace of Christ's cross, the fervent presence of the Holy Spirit, and unity as one body.

A Community That Grows Together

We desire to be God's community built up through the fellowship (koinonia) of the Holy Spirit, grounded in the gospel of Christ. Through shepherd small group communities and various gatherings, we share the Word and life together in fellowship.

Education & Training

Young Leader Development Track

The leadership model of Rodemnamu Church's Young Adults Ministry is the little shepherd who resembles the Good Shepherd, Jesus.

Training Programs

New Members Class (7 weeks)
An encounter with the gospel through seven themes from Genesis and the Gospel of John — Thirst, Hope, Freedom, Creation, Happiness, Gospel, Faith
Discipleship Class (12 sessions)
Encountering the discipleship of Jesus through the Gospel of Mark — Nature and Purpose of the Calling, Listening and God's Kingdom, Calling and Hope, Trials and Faith, Vision and Training, Historical Consciousness, Confession and Discipleship, Maturity, Lordship, Eschatology, Faith of the Cross, Resurrection Faith
Shepherd-in-Training (12 sessions)
Spiritual formation for developing a wholistic shepherd identity — Self-Identity, Self-Discipline, Personal Devotion and QT, Personal Bible Study, Prayer, Reading, Balanced Emotions, View of People & Relationships, One-on-One Nurturing (1), One-on-One Nurturing (2), Small Group Leadership, Christian Worldview

Other Programs

  • One-on-One Bible Study

    Personal Bible study with a willing partner at a convenient time

  • Book-by-Book Bible Study

    Pentateuch, John, Romans, Luke, Acts, and more

  • Doctrine Study

    Heidelberg Catechism, Calvin's Institutes, and more

  • Christian Worldview Training

    Seminar-style book discussions for Christian worldview formation

  • Topical Lectures

    Symposium for new students, marriage seminars, workplace preparation

  • LTC

    Leadership symposium for leaders and leader candidates

Seven Ministries

We hold the principle that being precedes doing, and aspire to become disciples of the Lord and little shepherds who resemble Christ. This service is expressed concretely in seven ways

Word & Nurture

Nurturing and caring for college-aged young adults through God's Word

Church Support

Supporting the worship and ministry of the Body of Christ

Educational Ministry

Educational support ministry for the next generation

Social Transformation

Restoring a suffering world and transforming every sphere of society

Reunification Mission

Serving toward the peaceful reunification of a divided Korean peninsula

Marginalized Communities

Serving international students, North Korean defectors, and the socially vulnerable

Global Mission

Joining God's mission unfolding simultaneously around the world
