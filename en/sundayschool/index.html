2025 Education Theme

"Learning the Love of Jesus"

2 Timothy 3:14–15

"But as for you, continue in what you have learned and have become convinced of, because you know those from whom you learned it, and how from infancy you have known the Holy Scriptures, which are able to make you wise for salvation through faith in Christ Jesus."

Education Goals

  • A department that learns and teaches God's Word

    From a young age, we learn and teach the Bible, growing up in the Word.

  • A department that joyfully plays and loves together

    Using both body and mind, we play together and grow strong in body and spirit.

  • A department that builds up faithful families

    Through the challenging season of parenting, we share concerns, encourage one another, and build up strong families of faith.

Key Programs

  • Children Who Worship

    Learning Bible stories through Sunday worship and deepening understanding through curriculum and activities. Practicing proper worship posture with teachers, learning to pray through offering prayers. Opening talent markets to motivate and inspire children.

  • Children Who Learn the Word

    Learning the Word through seasonal education (Easter, Thanksgiving, Christmas) and Winter/Summer Bible School. Celebrating birthdays on the last Sunday of each month, learning the preciousness of community.

  • Children Growing in Families

    Encouraging one another and sharing concerns through regular family gatherings. Supporting and helping families nurture faith at home.

Children's Department

Photo

Time / Location Sunday 11:00 AM | 5th Floor, Children's Room
Director Rev. Park Bong-il

2025 Education Theme

"Children of God Who Dream of the Kingdom of God"

Matthew 18:4

"Therefore, whoever takes the lowly position of this child is the greatest in the kingdom of heaven."

Education Goals

  • Authentic worship together with God
  • Discipleship that discovers what it means to be children of God's Kingdom
  • Fellowship that experiences the Kingdom of God together
  • Children living as witnesses of the Kingdom of God

Key Programs

  • Children Who Worship Wholeheartedly

    Hearing and practicing God's Word through narrative-based sermons and connected Bible study. Learning to actively worship God through public prayer and praise.

  • Children Who Learn Scripture and Doctrine

    Learning Old Testament narratives through small group activities. Learning doctrine centered on the Lord's Prayer and the Apostles' Creed through after-school gatherings.

  • Children in Community Fellowship

    Building community through after-school gatherings (indoor meetings and outdoor activities). Communal faith training and fellowship through Winter Bible School (February) and Summer Bible School (July–August).

  • Children Witnessing God's Kingdom

    Hosting friend invitation events twice a year together with the church community. Evangelism training through nurturing programs (Evangelism School, Gospel Cube) and Children's Ministry Mission Sundays.

Time / Location Sunday 11:00 AM | 1st Floor, Ruach
Director Ev. Yang Chang-geun

2025 Education Theme

"Living in the Kingdom of God"

Romans 14:17

"For the kingdom of God is not a matter of eating and drinking, but of righteousness, peace and joy in the Holy Spirit."

Education Goals

  • The Kingdom of God — together with Scripture
  • The Kingdom of God — together with the Church
  • The Kingdom of God — even at school
  • The Kingdom of God — even at home

Key Programs

  • Communal Faith Formation

    Community worship, small group Bible study, and birthday celebrations (community games, rolling paper). Training all members, including leaders, to serve with their given gifts. Personal quiet time training in daily life, and reading through the New Testament together by groups on Sundays.

  • Catechesis and Worldview Education

    Worldview-oriented catechesis using contemporary confessions of faith. Catechism for confirmation.

  • Discovering Calling and Vision

    Forming holistic identity through special lectures by faith mentors on academics and faith in school. Preparing peaceful character and capacity by praying together for unification every Sunday.
