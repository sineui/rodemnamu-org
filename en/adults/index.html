Adults Ministry

We hope that adults at the center of family, workplace, and church will continue to respond to Jesus' calling.

Community

The Adults Ministry is a community that shares God's Word and life together within family, workplace, and church, building one another up.

Worship Service

Sunday 11:00 AM  |  2nd Floor Worship Hall

Once a month, all generations gather together for a three-generational worship service. Baptism and Communion are administered.

Curriculum

New Believers

New Believers Class (7 weeks)

An encounter with the Gospel through seven themes from Genesis and John: thirsting, hope, freedom, creation, happiness, gospel, and faith.

Foundation

Bone Course

Seven foundational Christian truths — The Nature of God, Human Identity, Jesus Christ, The Holy Spirit, The Church, The Ten Commandments, Prayer

Deepening

Flesh Course

12 sessions — Scripture, Law & Gospel, True Faith, Providence, Forgiveness, Resurrection, Justification, Sacraments, Repentance, Worship, Piety, Blessing

Discipleship Training

Muscle Course

12 weeks — Identity, Renewing the Mind, Spiritual Disciplines, Worldview, Emotions, Will, Body, Church Relationships, Family, Cultural Engagement, Kingdom of God, World Mission

Seven Ministries

Word & Nurture

Nurturing and caring for adults through God's Word

Church Support

Supporting the worship and ministry of the Body of Christ

Educational Ministry

Educational support ministry for the next generation

Social Transformation

Restoring a suffering world and transforming every sphere of society

Reunification Mission

Serving toward the peaceful reunification of a divided Korean peninsula

Marginalized Communities

Serving international students, North Korean defectors, and the socially vulnerable

Global Mission

Joining God's mission unfolding simultaneously around the world
